## ❓ WHY NOT NETLIFY/VERCEL?

Netlify/Vercel are for **static sites only**:
- ❌ No Flask server
- ❌ No persistent database
- ❌ No 24/7 uptime

Your app needs a **real backend server**.

**Better options:**
1. Render (easiest) - $7/mo
2. Railway - $5/mo
3. Heroku - $7/mo
4. Google Cloud Run - pay-per-use
